What is Bitcoin
Bitcoin is a digital currency that is decentralized. Any two people can send each other bitcoins with no involvment of banks or governments.
Every transaction of bitcoins is recorded on the public ledger called blockchain. The ledger can be accessed by anyone.
There will only ever be 21 million bitcoins and since it's digital it cannot be inflated or manipulated. There is no need to acquire all bitcoins, owning a fraction suffices
