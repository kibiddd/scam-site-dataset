How quick do you deposit fiat payment?
We make Fiat payment in both Naira (NGN) and Pounds (GBP) immediately your digital asset is confirmed. We do not delay payment at all.
How do I contact you when I need help?
The best way to contact us for inquiry is to give us a call or send us a text on our whatsapp by clicking on the icon on the bottom right corner of the page
Are your Rates up to date?
In order to ensure that we always buy at the best market rate, we are regularly updating our rates as frequently as market price changes
What Payment methods do you operate?
We make payment for all your digital assets into any bank in Nigeria (NGN) or United Kingdom (GBP) of your choice. The details are collected in the order form.
What are your working hours?
We are always online 24/7 to trade with you. Our representatives are always available to provide needed assistance to complete transactions.
Do you trade alt coins?
Yes, we trade alt coins even if they are not listed on the order page. If you want to trade alt coins with us, contact us on whatsapp through the icon at the bottom right corner to get started.
