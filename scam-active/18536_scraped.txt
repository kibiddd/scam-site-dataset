What is a cryptocurrency wallet?
A cryptocurrency wallet is a medium that keeps the private and public keys needed for storing, transferring, and spending cryptocurrency. Most wallets are software, accessible online or through mobile and/or desktop applications, however there are some hardware wallets which provide users with a physical device. Crypto wallets may be exclusive for one coin or have multi-coin functionality. In order to purchase or to mine any cryptocurrency, users will need a wallet which will allow them to store said cryptocurrency first.
