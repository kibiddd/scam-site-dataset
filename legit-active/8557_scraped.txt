McIntosh County North Dakota Court Directory
The North Dakota trial court system consists of District Courts and Municipal Courts. For more information on which types of cases each court oversees, compare North Dakota courts.
Below is a directory of court locations in McIntosh County.  Links for online court records and other free court resources are provided for each court, where available. If you’re not sure which court you’re looking for, learn more about the North Dakota court system.
