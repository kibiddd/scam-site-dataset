Victoria, beloved wife of Ken Ridley and the late Duncan McTavish. Loved and devoted mother of Glen (Kristie), Ross (Trish), Beth (Jacqui) and Cameron (Karen). Cherished Gramma of Andrew (Melissa), Kelly (Conor), Adya (Paul), Spencer, Fraser and Simon. Great-grandmother of Angus. Special Mom to Ken's family, Garth (Heather), Patricia (Terry), David (Kathie) and grandson Blair. Survived by her sisters-in-law Vera Whelpdale and Jean Elgood. Special thanks to Dr. Parbal Sidhu and the nurses on 2E, Oakville-Trafalgar Memorial Hospital. Visitation will be held at the Kopriva Taylor Community Funeral Home, 64 Lakeshore Road West (one block east of Kerr, 905-844-2600), Oakville on Friday from 2-4 and 7-9 p.m. Funeral Service will be held 11:00 a.m. on Saturday, May 21, 2011 at St. Hilda's Anglican Church, 1258 Rebecca Street, Oakville. Cremation to follow. If desired, memorial contributions to Oakville-Trafalgar Memorial Hospital Foundation or Lymphoma Foundation Canada would be appreciated by the family.
