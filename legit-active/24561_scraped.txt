HorowitzSend FlowersFollowShareAboutUniversity of Rhode IslandMichael Horowitz Obituary(Asbury Park Press)MICHAEL J. HOROWITZAGE: 47 MIDDLETOWN Michael J. Horowitz, 47, of the Locust section of Middletown Twp., died Saturday, October 23, 2010, at home. Born in New York, He had lived in Locust for the past 14 years. Michael was a graduate of Christian Brothers Academy, Lincroft, and the University of Rhode Island .He was a communicant of Holy Cross Church, Rumson. Michael was a skilled sailor, an avid fisherman and a sports fan and a loyal generous friend. He was a member of the Shrewsbury River Yacht Club. Michael enjoyed a successful career as a floor trader on both the COMEX and the New York Mercantile Exchange, and was known by His "Myer" floor badge. His life was filled with world travel, adventures, insatiable curiosity and eclectic interests. More than anything he was a loving husband and father.He is survived by his wife, Patricia; son, Michael John, and daughter, Clare; his parents, Michael and Lucille; and his brother, Mark. Michael is also survived by many aunts, uncles, nieces, nephews, and his many friends.There will be a memorial gathering on Tuesday at The Thompson Memorial Home form 3-5 & 7-9pm. A Memorial Mass will be celebrated on Wednesday at Holy Cross Church, Ward Ave., Rumson, 10am.
