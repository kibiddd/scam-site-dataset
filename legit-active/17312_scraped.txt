Fan Death is string-driven synth pop collaboration, between Dandilion Wind Opaine (of Dandi Wind), Marta Jaciubek-McKeever & producer Szam Findlay - plus Marlene Ginader (violin) & Tony Dallas (drums). It was founded in Brooklyn, NY, USA (in 2007). To date, they have released two 12" records: "Veronica's Veil" on Erol Alkan's Phantasy Sounds imprint and Cannibal on Tokyo based  Big Love Records.
