West Coast Warzone 2 results and battle logs
Posted by Jon Catalyst Grey • April 5, 2010 at 12:46 a.m. PDT • Comments: 112
West Coast Warzone 2 has finished up, it was a very exciting tournament featuring some of the best players in California. Results for all of the Capcom fighting games have been posted.
There are also tournament brackets of the top 8 finishers and battle logs of the event so you can get more of a play by play of how things shook out.
For a whole bunch of coverage, just click the link below.
