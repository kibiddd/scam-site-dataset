Spread the metal:Heavy T.O. 2012
Live Nation has announce the return of Heavy T.O., on August 11th and 12th, 2012, to be held at Downsview Park in Toronto, ON.
Heavy MTL has also announce that its fourth edition will be held on August 11th and 12th, 201,2 at Parc Jean-Drapeau in Montreal.
Heavy MTL can be found online here. Check out Heavy T.O. online here.
Complete details to be announced.
