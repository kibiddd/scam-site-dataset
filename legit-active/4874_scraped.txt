Union Pacific Railroad Bridge, Riverside, California
Bridgepixing and Railfanning the Union Pacific Railroad Bridge (aka Santa Ana River Viaduct) in Riverside, California. This massive concrete arch railroad bridge, built 1904, spans the Santa Ana River. This Bridge can be accessed through the Martha McLean-Anza Narrows Park off Jurupa Ave. on the south side of the Santa Ana River.
