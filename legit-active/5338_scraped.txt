Jon Lajoie
Jonathan Lajoie (IPA: ) (born 1980) is a Canadian comedian, actor, and Internet celebrity from Montreal, Quebec. Lajoie was born and raised in Saint-Hubert, Quebec. His father is French-Canadian and his mother is English-Canadian. He graduated from Dawson College's theatre program in 2001.
