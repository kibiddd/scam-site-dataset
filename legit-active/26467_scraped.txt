(Ross) BrittainAGE: 85 WhitingReverend Donald R. (Ross) Brittain, 85, passed away Friday, Feb. 18, 2011, at his home in Whiting. Born and raised in Yarmouth, Nova Scotia, Ross resided in Colts Neck and Lakeville, Pa. before moving to Whiting in 2009. He was a Pastor ministering in Canada, PA, NJ and NY.Ross is survived by his wife of 60 years, Doris; his children, Diane Mahady (Paul) of Spring Lake, Rebecca Brittain of Caranavi, Bolivia, Donald Brittain Jr. (Karri) of Fredericksburg, Va. and their three children, Karli, Kimberly, Katie; and Jeffery Brittain (Ruth) of Chester, Pa., and their five children, Netanya, Jesse, Evelyn, Priscilla and Daniel. He is also survived by his sisters, Margaret Campbell of Yarmouth, Nova Scotia, Canada, Enid Langielle of Moncton, New Brunswick, Canada, Barbara Tutty of Hunts Point, Nova Scotia, Canada, and Elizabeth Smith of Windsor, Nova Scotia, Canada. Ross was predeceased by his oldest daughter, Beth Brittain, in 1975. A memorial service will be held at 11 a.m. Wednesday, Feb. 23 at the Whiting Bible Church, Lacey Road, Whiting. In lieu of flowers, donations can be made to the Cup of Cold Water Ministries, P.O. Box 318, Newark, IL 60541, designated for Life and Truth School Bolivia.
