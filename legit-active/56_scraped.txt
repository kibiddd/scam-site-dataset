Central American Zone (UNCAF Club Championship 2009)
Costa Rica Qualification:
Deportivo Saprissa as Apertura 2008 champions
Liberia as Clausura 2009 champions
Herediano as Clausura 2009 runners-up (replacing Nicaragua champions)
El Salvador Qualification:
Isidro Metapán as winners of Apertura 2008 and Clausura 2009
Luis Ángel Firpo (as Clausura 2009 runners-up, replaced Chalatenango
who had originally qualified but sold their top
level place in El Salvador to Municipal Limeño)
Guatemala Qualification:
Comunicaciones as Apertura 2008 champions
Deportivo Jalapa as Clausura 2009 champions
Honduras Qualification:
Marathón as Apertura 2008 champions
Olimpia as Clausura 2009 champions
Real España as Apertura 2008 and Clausura 2009 runners-up
Nicaragua Qualification:
Nicaraguan champions Real Estelí excluded (for not having a stadium
complying with conditions) and replaced by a third team from Costa
Rica (Herediano).
Panama Qualification:
Árabe Unido as Clausura 2008 champions
San Francisco as Apertura 2009 champions
Caribbean Zone Qualifying (CFU Club Champions Cup 2009)
NB: two clubs withdrew, among them Robinhood (Surinam); unclear why no clubs
from Jamaica enter
