Somewhere is a multi-disciplinary, non-profit creative organisation
run by artist / film-makers Nina Pope and Karen Guthrie
We produce ambitious projects spanning film, the web, installation, live events and public art. You can access most of our collaborative and solo work here, contact us, and read background information on what we do.
You can find out about our award-winning film, The Closer We Get and our latest public project This Is Enough.
