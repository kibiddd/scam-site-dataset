Cozmo
As the official 501(c)(3) non-profit arm of the Los Angeles Galaxy, the mission of the Los Angeles Galaxy Foundation (LAGF) is to advance the game of soccer and use it as a vehicle for positive change throughout Southern California communities. The organization has three main pillars that serve as the core of our granting and programming efforts: improving access to youth sports and health + wellness resources, while serving as champions for inclusion in our communities.
