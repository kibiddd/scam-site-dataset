Welcome to the Lowell Berry Foundation
The primary purpose of The Lowell Berry Foundation is to support organizations which are dedicated to strengthening leadership of the local Christian church ministry.  We also are committed to serving our local community by providing a safety net for those in need while focusing on organizations that effectively shape lives and build leaders for tomorrow.
