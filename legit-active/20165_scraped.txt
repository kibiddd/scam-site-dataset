Critical Mission Focus
Public Safety and Border Security
TRSS provides critical operational support by bringing together experts, unique data, and dynamic technology to facilitate efficient lead generation, vetting, and investigative analysis for high priority law enforcement missions.
National  Security
TRSS delivers actionable intelligence that exposes vulnerabilities across U.S. national security missions and programs. Whether your mission is assessing new threats, emerging technologies, barriers to collaboration, or staff readiness, TRSS supply chain solutions can deliver the critical information and insights you need.
Government Fraud Prevention
TRSS enables prosecution of large-scale fraud schemes by applying data science to proprietary public records data and technologies, surfacing both complex networks of transnational criminal organizations and actionable leads for law enforcement.
Commercial  Risk
TRSS Commercial Risk solutions anticipate and mitigate risk with enabled data and services that deliver actionable insights. Our flagship product LincsConnect, along with our unique data experts, ensures compliance and enables operational efficiencies and faster, smarter risk decisions.
