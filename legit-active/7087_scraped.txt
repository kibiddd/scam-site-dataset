Welcome to Golden Spike Harley-Davidson®
Since 1984 Intermountain Harley-Davidson® has been Utah’s premiere motorcycle dealership serving the Salt Lake City market. We offer the best new and used motorcycles in Utah. Sell us your used motorcycle, powersport machine or personal watercraft. All brands are accepted on trade toward the purchase of a new or used Harley-Davidson motorcycle. Visit our four Harley-Davidson dealerships in Northern Utah, including Golden Spike Harley-Davidson in Riverdale, for the complete line of Harley-Davidson motorcycles, MotorClothes and Genuine Harley-Davidson Parts.
