12-1 Bulgarian Cinema - Filmography DescriptionThis article is from the Bulgaria FAQ,
by Dragomir R. Radev radev@tune.cs.columbia.edu with numerous
contributions by others.12-1 Bulgarian Cinema - FilmographyThe whole PART II of Ronald Holloway's "The Bulgarian Cinema" is an
extensive filmography of Bulgarian cinema from 1915 to 1985. This list here
shoud be considered as an up-to-date appendix, though not comprehensive
by any means.
