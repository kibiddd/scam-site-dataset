Mary Judith Sieben was born March 24, 1941 in Minneapolis to Harry and Mary (Luger) Sieben. She was a 1959 graduate of Hastings High School and Creighton University in 1963. She was employed as a realtor with Remax for 20 years prior to retirement. Preceded by her parents, Judy is survived by her daughter, Bridget (Gerald) Whitcomb of Burlingame, CA; sons, John "Jack" (Anne) McGill of Scottsdale, AZ and Robert McGill of Maitland, FL; grandchildren, Samantha, Isabella, Abigail, and Patrick; siblings, Harry "Tex" (Ginny) Sieben of Hastings, Michael (Gretchen) Sieben of Woodbury, William (Joyce) Sieben of Hastings, John (Susan) Sieben of Woodbury, and Margaret "Missy" (Fred) Schmidt of Eagan. The Funeral Mass, officiated by Fr. James Perkl, will be held 9:30 am Saturday at St. Elizabeth Ann Seton Catholic Church, 2035 W. 15th St., Hastings, with visitation 1 hour prior to service. Interment will follow in the parish cemetery. Memorials are preferred to the Alzheimer's Association. or American Cancer Society.
