Slamboree 1993
"A Legends Reunion"
May 23, 1993 in Atlanta, GA
The Omni drawing 7,008 ($37,000)
Shown live on PPV (0.5)
Legends Introduction: Ole Anderson, The Assassin (Hamilton), Ox Baker, Red Bastein, James Blears, Crusher, Fabulous Moolah, Greg Gagne, Verne Gagne, Bob Geigle, Stu Hart, Magnum TA, Bugsy McGraw, Don Owen, Dusty Rhodes, Grizzly Smith, Lou Thesz, John Tolos, Maurice Vachon, Johnny Valentine, and Mr. Wrestling II.
Too Cold Scorpio & Marcus Bagwell beat Bobby Eaton & Chris Benoit. (9:22) when Scorpio pinned Benoit.
Sid Vicious pinned Van Hammer (0:35).
Dick Murdoch, Don Muraco, & Jimmy Snuka NC Wahoo McDaniel, Blackjack Mulligan, & Jim Brunzell (9:06).
Thunderbolt Patterson & Brad Armstrong beat Ivan Koloff & Baron Von Raschke (4:39) when Patterson pinned Raschke.
Dory Funk, Jr. (seconded by Gene Kiniski) drew Nick Bockwinkel (seconded by Verne Gagne) (15:00).
Rick Rude & Paul Orndorff beat Dustin Rhodes & Kensuke Sasaki (9:25) when Rude pinned Sasaki.
Sting pinned The Prisoner (5:16).
WCW Hall of Fame Inductions: Lou Thesz, Mr. Wrestling II, Verne Gagne, and Eddie Graham (posthumous).
WCW Tag Champs Brian Pillman & Steve Austin beat Dos Hombres (Steamboat & Zenk) (16:08) in a "steel cage" match when Austin pinned Zenk.  Zenk was supposed to be Shane Douglas.
NWA World Champ Barry Windham pinned Arn Anderson (10:55).
WCW World Champ Big Van Vader beat Davey Smith (16:16) via DQ.

Slamboree 1994
"A Legends Reunion"
May 22, 1994 in Philadelphia, PA
Civic Center drawing 4,000 ($53,000)
Shown live on PPV (0.48)
Paul Orndorff & Paul Roma beat Brian & Brad Armstrong.
Legends Introduction:  Ole Anderson, Penny Banner, Red Bastein, Tully Blanchard, The Crusher, Don Curtis, Terry Funk, Verne Gagne, Hard Boiled Haggerty, Larry Hennig, Killer Kowalski, Ernie Ladd, Wahoo McDaniel, Angelo Mosca Sr, Harley Race, Ray Stevens, Lou Thesz, Mr. Wrestling II, and Tommy Young.
WCW U.S. Champ Steve Austin pinned Johnny B. Badd (16:12).
Terry Funk DDQ Tully Blanchard (7:15).
Larry Zbysko pinned Steven Regal (11:30).
Dustin Rhodes pinned Bunkhouse Buck (12:47) in a "bullrope" match.
WCW World Champ Ric Flair pinned Barry Windham (13:21).
WCW Hall of Fame Inductions:  The Assassin (Jody Hamilton), Ole Anderson, Harley Race, Ernie Ladd, The Crusher, and Dick the Bruiser (posthumous).
Cactus Jack & Kevin Sullivan beat The Nasty Boys (9:56) in a "broadstreet bully" match to win the WCW Tag Title when Cactus pinned Sags.  Dave Shultz (NHL player) was the special referee.
Sting pinned Vader (13:54) to win the vacant WCW International World Title.

Slamboree 1998
May 17, 1998 in Worcester, MA
The Centrum drawing 11,592 ($352,035)
Shown live on PPV (0.72)
WCW TV Champ Fit Finlay pinned Chris Benoit (14:52).
Lex Luger beat Bryan Adams (5:05) via submission.
Ciclope (Dean Malenko) won a "cruiserweight battle royal" (8:27).  Also in the match were: Evan Courageous, Damien, Dandy, El Grio, Juventud Guerrera, Chavo Guerrero Jr., Marty Jannetty, Billy Kidman, Lenny Lane, Psicosis, Super Calo, Johnny Swinger, and Villano IV.

WCW U.S. Champ Bill Goldberg pinned Saturn (7:01).
Bret Hart beat Randy Savage (16:38) via submission.  The decision was reversed the next night on Nitro by guest referee Roddy Piper.
