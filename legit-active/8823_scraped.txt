Jacksonville Sharks fall to Arizona Rattlers in season openerThe Times-UnionPHOENIX - The Arizona Rattlers scored 22 fourth-quarter points and defeated the Jacksonville Sharks 55-52 in a season-opening Arena Football League game Saturday night.Sharks quarterback Aaron Garcia completed 24-of-39 passes for 259 yards, five touchdowns and two interceptions.Jeron Harvey caught nine passes for 110 yards and two TDs for Jacksonville. Jomo Wilson had seven catches for 86 yards and three touchdowns for the Sharks.The Sharks play their home opener at 8 p.m. Friday against the Georgia Force at Jacksonville Veterans Memorial Arena.
