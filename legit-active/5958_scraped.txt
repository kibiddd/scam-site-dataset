He was born in Greenfield, Iowa on February 28, 1923. William served in World War II as a Merchant Marine and moved to Des Moines, Iowa where he had a 26 year career as an IRS agent.  He and his wife Hilda retired and moved to Yuma in 1980.  William enjoyed travel and history and was an avid Catholic. William is survived by his brother, James Huston of Wisconsin; son, Michael (Celia) Huston of California; daughters, Carol (Walter)Bickford of Yuma and Teresa (Dennis) Boroczk of Texas; grandchildren, Stacey (Matt) Tams and Billy Huston; and great-grandchildren, Drew Tams and Addison Tams. William was preceded in death by his wife of 59 years, Hilda, his sister, Margery Freas and his companion,   Mercedes Christensen.A graveside service will be held at Sunset Vista Cemetery, Yuma on Friday, April 2, 2010 10:00 am.
