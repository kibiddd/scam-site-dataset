Uncle Scrooge Adventures in Color
by William Van Horn
William Van Horn is widely acclaimed for his madcap duck tales, from one-page gags to book-length stories of high adventure, with a particular knack for the Walt Disney's Comics and Stories ten-pager. Gladstone is proud to present this four-volume album series collecting the early works (published during1988-90) of this all-time great modern Disney comics writer/artist!
