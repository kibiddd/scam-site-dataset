The 1988 Oakland Athletics played 162 games during the regular season, won 104 games, lost 58 games, and finished in first position. They played their home games at Oakland Coliseum (Park Factors: 97/95) where 2,287,335 fans witnessed their 1988 Athletics finish the season with a .642 winning percentage.
