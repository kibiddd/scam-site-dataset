The 1990 Oakland Athletics played 162 games during the regular season, won 103 games, lost 59 games, and finished in first position. They played their home games at Oakland Coliseum (Park Factors: 96/95) where 2,900,217 fans witnessed their 1990 Athletics finish the season with a .636 winning percentage.
