Founded In
Toronto, Ontario, Canada
Winter Rose are a Canadian glam rock band, most notable for being the original band of highly-acclaimed singer James LaBrie. The band released their self-titled debut with LaBrie on vocals, before he successfully auditioned for the vacant vocalist position in American progressive metal heavyweights Dream Theater.
