About this litter
ONYX Hollowshot Trippin' On Sunshine, RPT, ADD, ADDCh, NGC, CGC, TDI, NA
x Imp. Dru RPT, BDD, DD, CGC, FMX
Born August 4, 2006There were four females and one male in this litter. They are high drive pups that LOVE to perform in everything including Flyball, Agility and Disc Dog. At home they are all calm loving companions who are great at keeping the couch warm.  With Tripp being their daddy these puppies are also major water lovers and can barely be kept out of the water!  Buddy, Breeze and Karma have been spayed/neutered.  Stash and Juice have been left intact for breeding purposes.These puppies are CBCA and AKC reg'd
