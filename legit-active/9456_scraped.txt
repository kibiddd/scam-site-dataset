Born 1916 in Tucson, AZ. Preceded in death by his beloved wife on 70 years, Celestina; daughter, Olga; sons, Manny and Tommy. He is survived by daughters, Kathy Olivas Mendez (Ray) and Sylvia Olivas Edwards (Fred); son, Enrique Romo (Shirley); sister, Josephine Olivas Benitez; brother, Gilbert Olivas; stepbrother, Jose Olivas, Rudy Olivas and Walter Olivas; 11 grandchildren, 27 great-grandchildren, seven great-great-grandchildren and many nieces and nephews. He was dearly loved and will be deeply missed by all who knew him. He will be in our hearts forever. Services on Tuesday, January 18, 2011 at St. Margaret's Church, 801 N. Grande Ave. Visitation-Rosary 9:30 a.m. - 10:30 a.m., Mass 10:30 a.m. - 11:30 a.m. Burial to follow at Holy Hope Cemetery, Oracle/Prince.
