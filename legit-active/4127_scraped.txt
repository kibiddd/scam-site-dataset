La Vérendrye (redirected from La Verendrye)
La Vé·ren·drye  (lä vā-räN-drē′), Sieur de Title of  Pierre Gaultier de Varennes. 1685-1749. French-Canadian explorer who established a chain of trading posts in New France, thus breaking Britain's virtual monopoly on the Canadian fur trade.American Heritage® Dictionary of the English Language, Fifth Edition.
