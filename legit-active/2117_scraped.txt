Update Your Browser
Browser Update Required
Your browser is out of date. Please update to the latest version to ensure the best performance and security.
How to Update Your Browser
Google Chrome
Open Chrome.
Click on the three dots in the upper-right corner of the browser window.
Go to "Help" and then "About Google Chrome."
Chrome will automatically check for updates and install them.
After the update, click "Relaunch."
Download Chrome
Mozilla Firefox
Open Firefox.
Click on the three lines in the upper-right corner of the browser window.
Go to "Help" and then "About Firefox."
Firefox will automatically check for updates and install them.
After the update, click "Restart to update Firefox."
Download Firefox
Microsoft Edge
Open Edge.
Click on the three dots in the upper-right corner of the browser window.
Go to "Help and feedback" and then "About Microsoft Edge."
Edge will automatically check for updates and install them.
After the update, click "Restart."
Download Edge
Apple Safari
Open the App Store.
Click on "Updates" in the left-hand sidebar.
Look for Safari in the list of available updates.
Click on "Update."
Download Safari
