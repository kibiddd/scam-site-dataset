2002 Montreal Expos Roster | Research by Baseball Almanac
The 2002 Montreal Expos played 162 games during the regular season, won 83 games, lost 79 games, and finished in second position. They played their home games at Stade Olympique (Park Factors: 101/101) where 812,536 fans witnessed their 2002 Expos finish the season with a .512 winning percentage.
