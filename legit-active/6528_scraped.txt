King Khan & the Shrines, sometimes referred to as King Khan and (His) Sensational Shrines, is a Berlin-based garage rock and psychedelic soul band. Who is this maniac Master of Ceremonies who openly confesses to the two grand black bohemians said to be lunatic Sun Ra and George Clinton, and whose stagings certainly remind us of a young Herman Sonny Blount and his Solar Arkestra? In 1995 Khan leaves the frosty soil of the city of Montreal playing the bass for the International-Underground-Legends THE SPACESHITS.
