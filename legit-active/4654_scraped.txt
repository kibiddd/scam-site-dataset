Below is a list of names from my database (over 6000 names) that I compiled (years of work) from camp records, newspapers, books, visits to local cemeteries, etc.
Most of the names are from New York City Cemeteries such as Evergreen, Lutheran, Greenwood, Woodlawn, Calvary, Cypress Hills and many more cemeteries. Please e-mail me if you want information on a listed name. I can give you all the information I have on the listed veteran. Please limit 2 requests per week. I can add your ancestors name (rank, regiment, date of death, cemetery) to my database and post it below.
Send your request or addition to SVC George J.Weinmann.
