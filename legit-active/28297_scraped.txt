Born December 28, 1929 in Aiken, SC, to Clifford Chapman Johnson Mary Cecil Holstein Johnson, Ann grew up in Spartanburg, SC. She graduated from the Salem Academy in Winston-Salem, NC, attended the College at St. Mary's in Raleigh, NC, and graduated with a Bachelor of Science degree from the Richmond Professional Institute at the College of William & Mary in Virginia. In a day when professional women in the workplace were rare, Ann worked in the buying office of Thalhimers, then taught elementary school, and later owned and successfully operated her own specialty boutique, Ann's of Ft. Walton Beach, FL.  Along with her late husband, Robert L. Koenig, M.D., Ann raised one son and four daughters. The family grew up in Marshall, TX, where Ann managed her husband's medical practice until his death in 1995. She was actively involved in the Marshall Medical Auxiliary, the Trinity Episcopal Church, and the Symphony League of Marshall. Ann is survived by her husband, Albert David McPhail; her children and sons-in-law, Robert L. Koenig, Jr. of Mansfield, TX, Kathy Koenig of Longview, TX, Karen Koenig and John Taylor of Marshall, TX, Laura Koenig and Michael Young of Tyler, TX, and Susan Koenig and Mark Thomas of Arlington, TX; her sister and brother-in-law, Mary Clifford and Jan Whitehurst of Tryon, NC, as well as nieces and nephew, Kim Whitehurst, Amy Whitehurst King, and Lee Whitehurst. Ann also proudly looked after her grandchildren, Chendo Gonzalez, Daniel Gonzalez. Bradford Maddox, Blake Maddox, Michael Ann Young, Caroline Young, Taylor Maddox, and Kristen Thomas as well as her great-grandchildren, Leo Gonzalez, Dylan Gonzalez, Riley Gonzalez, Haylee Maddox and Sam Gonzalez. She was also stepmother to Leslie Peterson, Amy Bartell and Douglas McPhail. Ann will be missed by all who loved her, and whose lives she touched and helped to shape.  A celebration and remembrance of the life of Laura Ann Johnson Koenig McPhail will be held at 11:00 a.m. Friday, August 19, 2011 at Trinity Episcopal Church in Marshall followed by a private burial with the family at Colonial Gardens. Visitation will be Thursday, August 18, 2011 from 6:00 p.m. to 8:00 p.m. at Sullivan Funeral Home in Marshall. The Honorary Pallbearers will be Tony Bridge, Pat Furrh, Scotty Baldwin, Scott Baldwin, Buddy Young, Bill Palmer, Jan Whitehurst, Royal Becker, M.D., Harry Buchanan, Jim Talley, Jack Herrin, and Jack Sanders. Pallbearers will be Bradford Maddox, Blake Maddox, Taylor Maddox, Chendo Gonzalez, Daniel Gonzalez, and Lee Whitehurst. Remembrances, in lieu of flowers, may be made to Trinity Episcopal Church, 106 North Grove Street, Marshall, TX 75670; Trinity Mother Frances Foundation, 800 East Dawson, Tyler, TX, 75701; or The Hospice of East Texas, 4111 University Boulevard, Tyler, TX, 75701. Online condolences may be made to www.sullivan-funeralhome.com.
