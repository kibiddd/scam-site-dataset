Louie enjoyed hunting and fishing, and was a member of the LTHS track team. Visitation Wednesday 4 to 8 p.m. and Thursday 3 to 9 p.m., at  Hallowell & James Funeral Home, 1025 W. 55th St., Countryside. Interment Private. Family and friends to gather on Sunday, April 10, for a 3 p.m. Memorial Service, at the First Presbyterian Church of LaGrange, 150 So. Ashland Ave., LaGrange IL 60525. If desired, memorials may be directed to the Lymphoma Research Foundation,the Leukemia and Lymphoma Society or Bear Necessities Pediatric Cancer Foundation.
