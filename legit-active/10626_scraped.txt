She was preceded in death by her parents, Sam Sr. and Lovey Smelser. She was a member of Glade Creek Lutheran Church where she was a member of the Church Choir, the LCW (Lutheran Church Women's Organization) who made quilts for the Rescue Mission and chocolate Easter Eggs for Charity Fund Raising, and taught Sunday School. She retired as Assistant Manager of the Woolco Department Store after 17 years of service. She is survived by her loving husband of 64 years, Harley O. (Buddy) Baker Jr.; two sons and daughters-in-law, Dean and Emmy Baker, and Ronald O. and Terry C. Baker, all of Blue Ridge; three grandchildren, Jerry Wayne Baker and wife, Jessica B. Baker, of New Mexico, Savannah and Chelsea Broce; two great-grandchildren, Madison and Mason Baker; one brother, Samuel Smelser Jr.; one aunt, Selma Spickard; and many nieces and nephews. The family would like to thank the Carilion Roanoke Memorial Hospital Palliative Care Unit for the comfort and care given to Mary Jane. She will be missed greatly by who knew and loved her. A Funeral Service will be held at 11 a.m. on Thursday, October 6, 2011, at Oakey's East Chapel with the Rev. Kenneth Lane officiating. Interment will follow in Glade Creek Cemetery. Those wishing to make a memorial contribution, please consider the Glade Creek Lutheran Church.
