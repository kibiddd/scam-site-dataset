How to Use Fastwax
The secret is out! This is your car’s new best friend. After being a long time secret of professional racers, you can now wash and detail your car at the comforts of your own home. Say goodbye to the dirt and grime and give your vehicle a professional show car finish with Fastwax!
FW1 is a high performance cleaning wax that is designed not only to wax your vehicle but also remove grease, tar, bugs, mildew, road grime, light paint marks, oxidation and tree sap without using water! FW1 contains no silicone or CFC’s
HOW TO USE:
Shake can for 10 – 15 seconds.
Spray on 3 – 5 sq. ft. area.
Clean with a soft terry cloth towel. Allow to haze.
Polish with a microfiber towel.

FW1 is a high performance cleaning wax that is designed to clean vehicles without the use of water. You can achieve fast, amazing results in a matter of seconds.
FW1 removes grease, tar, bugs, mildew, road grime, light scratches and marks, most faded paints and free sap - in a matter of seconds.
FW1, which is made in USA, has been approved with a Smart Approved Watermark from the Australian Government to show it satisfies water-reduction criteria. Considering it takes about 150 gallons of water to wash one car, but only one can of FW1 to wash four medium sized cars, saving one billion gallons of water per year and counting.
