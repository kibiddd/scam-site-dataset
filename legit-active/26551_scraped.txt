Stephan T. Lawson, 13, Danville, passed away on October 23, 2008.Born Aug. 4, 1995 in Danville, he was the son of Brandon K. and Tammera D. Hoop Lawson. Stephan was a seventh-grade student at Danville Community Middle School.He was active in basketball, baseball and a member of the Hendricks County 4-H Bridle Club.Survivors include his parents, Brandon and Tammy Lawson, Anthony Bruce, siblings: Tacia and Jeffrey Mullen, Danny and Charlie Bruce, Tance and Tarron Lawson, grandparents: Mike and Jody Siddons, Steve and Betty Hoop, Tom and Judy Tyler and Jack and Connie Lawson, great grandparents: Harry and Deloris Hoop, Naomi Mynatt and Lou Whitaker; and several Aunts, Uncles, Cousins and especially his cousin, friend and soulmate, Chelsie Gould.Services will be 11 a.m. on Monday, Oct. 27 in the Hendricks County 4-H Fairgrounds Conference Center, with the Reverend Gary Black officiating.Entombment will follow in the Danville South Cemetery Mausoleum.Visitation will be from 2 to 8 p.m. on Sunday, Oct. 26 in Baker Funeral Home, Danville.Contributions may be made to the Hendricks County Animal Shelter, 945 E. Main St. Danville, IN 46122.Online condolences may be made at www.bakerfuneralservice.com
