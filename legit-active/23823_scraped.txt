Needham Broughton High School
Class Of 1989
The Class of 1989 celebrated their 35th Reunion on Saturday, October 19th, 2024 at Bowstring in Raleigh.  An incredible amount of fun and fellowship was had, which this class has never had trouble doing together through the years. Everyone picked up right where they left off.  We missed those who could not join us, especially those who left us too soon.  Our class also graciously gave some much-needed funds to those in Western North Carolina who have lost so much.  For anyone who missed the event, just know that you were thought of.  Be sure to bookmark this website, as this is how we will communicate future gatherings, additional news and pictures related to our class and of course our next reunion which you will NOT want to miss.  Also, please be sure to Like and Follow our Facebook page entitled Broughton High School Class of 89 for other important communication from our classmates.  Here are more pictures from the evening at Bowstring: https://markbrocker.smugmug.com/Random/Broughton-Class-of-1989-Reunion/n-98TRZK
We will hold our 40th Reunion sometime in 2029.
