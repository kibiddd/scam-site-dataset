About Creative
Creative Deck & Vinyl Products is Maryland's leading deck and fence company. We produce, retail, and install products such as decking ,railing, fencing, patios, sunrooms and pergolas. You've come to the one stop shop because no matter your position as DIYer, contractor or homeowner Creative Deck & Vinyl Products can help you with the needed products and/or installation service. By doing it all we have mastered the industry and are ready to take you from step A to Z on your project.
Our warehouse and showroom are ALWAYS fully stocked with a large assortment of colors and styles. Start with Creative Deck & Vinyl Products when you want the best selection and value, backed by over 30 years of perfecting the Industry. Visit our showroom today!
