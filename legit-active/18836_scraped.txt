In his new book, Harmony: A New Way of Looking At Our World (HarperCollins, November 2)—discussed by Bob Colacello in the November issue of the U.S. edition of Vanity Fair—the heir to the British throne definitively, and fervently, vocalizes his thoughts about the planet and its future. Examining everything from architecture to organic farming to sustainable economics, His Royal Highness presents a compelling case that modern industrialization has led to a state of disharmony with nature.
