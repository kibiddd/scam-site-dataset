KurtzmanSend FlowersFollowShareKelly Kurtzman ObituaryKelly J. KurtzmanCRESTLINE: Kelly J. Kurtzman, 41, of 1001 Clink Blvd., Crestline, died Thursday, August 4, 2011 at Hospice House, Ashland. She had been ill since January. Kelly was born in Crestline on January 19, 1970 to Paul E. and Donna J. (Klein) Algire, Crestline. She married Stephen C. Kurtzman on November 26, 1993 and he survives.Kelly was a member of St. Joseph Catholic Church, Crestline. She was the City Auditor of Crestline for six years and had served as City Auditor of Galion for two years. With her sister-in-law Babette Ronk, she coached with the first Crestline Girls Junior Softball League Team. Kelly was elected to the Crestline Sports Hall of Fame in 2010. She was an avid reader and loved to scrapbook. She was very active with her children's sports. She really, really, really loved her shoes. She is survived by her son, Connor Kurtzman and daughter Karlie Kurtzman both at home; her father-in-law and mother-in-law, Louis & Margaret Kurtzman, Crestline; one sister and four brothers, Kim Schroeder of Galion, Rick & Babette Ronk, Crestline; Dick & Deb Algire of Mt. Gilead, Doug & Linda Algire, York, SC; and Ted & Shirley Algire, Hong Kong; nieces and nephews: Heather & Dave Snyder, Chad Schroeder, Amanda & Trevor Moyer, Meghan Ronk, Natalie Ronk, Louie Kurtzman, Sadie Kurtzman, Brandie & Andy McBrayer, Brooke Yosick, Jonah Kurtzman, Landon Kurtzman, Jennifer Algire Andrews, Lynlee & Pete Dzugan, Andy Algire, Adrian Algire, Michael Algire, Aimee Algire, Christine Wilson, Kathleen Fedarcyk, Ted Algire Jr., great nieces & great nephews Cameron Snyder, Makenna Moyer, Addison McBrayer, Morgan McBrayer, Brody Hatfield, Theresa Dzugan, and Peyton Dzugan; brothers-in-law & sister-in-law Bob and Anne Kurtzman, and Brian Kurtzman, and grandfather Robert Meier, Crestline. She was preceded in death by her brother Jeff Ronk and nephew Ricky Yosick.
Friends may call Sunday, August 7, 2011 from 4-8 PM at the St. Joseph Convocation Center, Crestline. A Mass of Christian Burial will be celebrated at 10:30 AM Monday, August 8, 2011 at Saint Joseph Catholic Church, Crestline, with Rev. Michael A. Geiger, Celebrant. Burial will follow in Saint Joseph Catholic Cemetery, Crestline.Memorial contributions may be made to Hospice House of Hospice of North Central Ohio, Ashland, in care of the funeral home.
