He was the husband of the late Grace (Oldford) Montefusco. Born in Providence, he was the son of the late Joseph and Nancy (Squillante) Montefusco. He worked as a Final Calibrationist for BIF/General Signal for 28 years. He was a member of the Primer Cordeiro VFW Post 5385. He is survived by his children, Tina Stevens of Dayville, CT, Antonio J. Montefusco of Chepachet and 4 grandchildren. He is survived by Adeline DiRocco of Johnston, Carol Galato and Nancy Giordano both of Warwick, Domenic Montefusco and Joseph Montefusco both of Coventry. He was the brother of the late Filomena D'Attilio, Antoinetta Marques and Florence, Ralph and Peter Montefusco. His funeral will begin on Tuesday at 9 AM from the REBELLO FUNERAL HOME, 901 Broadway, East Providence, RI. A Mass of Christian Burial will follow at 10 AM in St. Martha's Church, 2595 Pawtucket Ave., East Providence. Burial will be in Gate of Heaven Cemetery.
