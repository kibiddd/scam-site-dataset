Machulak, Robertson & Sodos, S.C.
WELCOME
Machulak, Robertson  & Sodos, S.C. is a general practice firm with offices located on the east  side of Milwaukee and in Brookfield, Wisconsin.   Our firm is committed to providing comprehensive quality legal services  to our clients and to finding cost-effective solutions to their needs.  We serve a diverse group of clients, both  individuals and corporations, many of whom have been with the firm for more  than twenty years.  Our areas of practice  include civil and commercial litigation, construction disputes, labor law,  constitutional, tort, family law, criminal law and personal injury.  Throughout the 25 years that the firm has  been in existence, we have worked hard to earn trust and respect within the  community by not only being zealous advocates for our clients, but also by  being ethical practitioners of the law.
