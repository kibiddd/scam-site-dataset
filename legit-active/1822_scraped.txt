Carole A. WalshWOONSOCKET- Carole A. Walsh 64, of Gaulin Ave. died Sunday at Landmark Medical Center.She was born in Woonsocket a daughter of the late George and Anita (Mondoux) Walsh.Carole was a graduate of The Sawyer School. She was employed as a medical assistant at Thundermist Health Center for over seventeen years.She is survived by her sister, Linda Walsh-Merlan of Woonsocket, and her companion of fifty years, Roland Bergeron of Woonsocket. Carole was Auntie to six nieces and nephews, and Great Aunt to many. She was also the sister of the late Ronald Walsh and Sandi Doiron.Her funeral will be held Thursday at 10:00 AM from the Fournier & Fournier Funeral Home, 463 South Main St. with a service starting at 11:00 AM. Burial will be private. Visiting hours are Wednesday 2-4 and 6-8 PM. In lieu of flowers contributions to Because He Lives Ministries, 298 Blackstone St. Woonsocket, RI 02895 in her memory would be appreciated.Please visitwww.fournierandfournier.comfor guestbook and directions.
