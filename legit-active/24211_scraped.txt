The 2003 Kansas City Royals played 162 games during the regular season, won 83 games, lost 79 games, and finished in third position. They played their home games at Kauffman Stadium (Park Factors: 113/112) where 1,779,895 fans witnessed their 2003 Royals finish the season with a .512 winning percentage.
