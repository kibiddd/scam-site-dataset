BethuneSend FlowersFollowShareJohn Bethune ObituaryJohn B. Bethune Fort Gratiot John B. Bethune, Age 90 of Fort Gratiot, formerly of Wyandotte Michigan. Went home with the Lord on Thursday, October 6, 2010; surrounded by his family. John was the fifth of six children born to John and Bertha (Fairhall) Bethune on May 22, 1921. John was born in Brantford, Ontario Canada; his parents moved the family to Wyandotte when John was 5, on August 9, 1926. He married "The love of his life" Ruth Wyke on September 19, 1947 in Wyandotte. She passed away on August 18, 1999 after 52 years together. John is survived by his three daughters, Beverly Krause, Nancy (Bill) Senkow and Fran (Gilbert) Flotte. 8 grandchildren, 21 great grandchildren and a brother-in-law John (Barbara) Wyke. John Proudly served his country during WWII in the Philippines and New Guinea. John joined the Wyandotte Police Department on January 16, 1950 and retired on January 18, 1980. He was the oldest sergeant the police department ever had. He was also past captain of the pistol team for 18 years. He was a life member of the VFW Post 1136-Wyandotte, Disable Veterans of America, FOP, past president of the Wayne County Police Lieutenants, Sergeants and Corporals Association and the Masonic Lodge in Wyandotte and Port Huron. John was an avid bowler and golfer. He loved the Red Wings and Tigers. He and Ruth enjoyed camping and traveled thru all 50 states (except Alaska and Hawaii) in their trailer. They also had a cabin in Farwell for 20 years. His family honors the memory of John and invites you to visit and share memories Tuesday October 11, 2011: 1-8 p.m. at the R.J.Nixon Funeral Home 2544 Biddle Ave, Wyandotte, with a VFW Salute at 6 p.m. and Masonic service at 7 p.m. Funeral Services Wednesday 11am from the funeral home. Pallbearers will be Brian Thivierge, Frank Krause, Eric Krause, Erick Krause, T.J.
