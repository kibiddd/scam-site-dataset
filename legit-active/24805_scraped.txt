McDonald, Jr. Funeral Home403 West Main StreetGirardville, PennsylvaniaPatrick Monaghan ObituaryJune 4, 2011Patrick Robert "Nuf" Monaghan, 83, of 430 Mount Olive Blvd., Lost Creek, passed away Saturday at Schuylkill Medical Center-East Norwegian Street.Born in Lost Creek, he was a son of the late Thomas and Nellie Monaghan.He was a retired pipefitter and a member of Local 524 Union.He was a member of St. Mary Magdalen Roman Catholic Church, Lost Creek.He was preceded in death by his wife, Patricia "Trisha" Wood Monaghan, in 2009; three brothers, Thomas, Francis and William Monaghan.Surviving are a son, Robert "Nuf" Monaghan and his wife, Tricia, Exton; two daughters, Anne and her husband, Harry Murray, Raven Run, and Ginny Monaghan, Philadelphia; a sister, Rosemary Wynne, Philadelphia; two grandchildren, Bobby Monaghan and Alexa Marie Murray; nieces and nephews.Funeral services will be held at 9:30 a.m. Wednesday at William F. McDonald Jr. Funeral Home, Girardville. Mass of Christian Burial will be celebrated at 10 a.m. Wednesday at Annunciation BVM Roman Catholic Church, Shenandoah. Interment will be in St. Joseph Cemetery, Fountain Springs. A viewing will be held from 6 to 9 p.m. today ﻿ at the funeral home. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to Juvenile Diabetes Foundation, 120 Wall St., 19th floor, New York, NY 10005. William F.
