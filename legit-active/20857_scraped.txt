Send Flowers Join our obituary email list Search all obituariesThank you for visiting our website.Our goal is to provide you with information, advice and assistance on matters related to funeral service. The William F. Conroy Funeral Home, Inc. is located in the Sheraden neighborhood of Pittsburgh. The funeral home was established on July 4, 1940 by William F. Conroy (1913-1946). It was his desire to serve families with care and compassion as they cope with a loss. For over 80 years, we still maintain that same desire and continually strive to hold the community’s trust and confidence.
