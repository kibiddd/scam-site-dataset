Festival Schedule RevealedPavement, LCD Soundsystem, the National, Vampire Weekend, MGMT, and more.By Tom BreihanMay 24, 2010Save this storySaveSave this storySaveWe're inching closer and closer to Memorial Day Weekend, the unofficial beginning of summer and also the weekend of the Sasquatch! Festival, taking place May 29-31 at the Gorge in George, Washington. (Yes, George, Washington. Ha ha, LOL, etc.) This year's fest has a ton of big names: Pavement, LCD Soundsystem, MGMT, Vampire Weekend, the National, Broken Social Scene, the xx, and plenty more. It also features comedians like Patton Oswalt, Rob Riggle, Craig Robinson, and Todd Barry.We've got a handy little guide after the jump, pointing you toward the acts that Pitchfork recommends.
