LIONFOX MASTERING INFO
We will create a take care of all your final master requirements with excellent service.All necessary and current formats will be provided.Please specify any deadlines nad we are happy to honor them, any amount of time.Masters are priced dynamically based on needs.A typical 12 song CD will cost around $600 (give or take) to master.If you have just a individual track it will cost $100 (instrumental and alternate versions are included)All mastered files provided with art and metadata/ISRC #s  that you provide us.Text information will be embedded in the master as MetaData used for reports that are generated. It is very important to include the proper spelling for:CD title:CD Artist:Song Titles:Song order.Mastered Tracks are delivered    via Samply App,dropbox, or wetranfer.
