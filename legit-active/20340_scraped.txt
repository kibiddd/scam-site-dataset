Welcome to the Crowne Plaza Lyon Hotel, where conviviality and comfort come together to offer you an unforgettable experience. Located in the heart of the 6th district, within the Cité Internationale, this establishment is the ideal place to relax and enjoy all the wonders of Lyon, 10 minutes from the city centre and the Part-Dieu train station. Public transport and bicycles can be rented in front of the hotel, while car and bicycle parking is at the foot of the property (fees apply).
When you enter, you are immediately greeted by a warm and modern decor. Whether you are here for business or pleasure,the Crowne Plaza Lyon hotel is the ideal place for you, the caring and dedicated team is here to meet all your needs.
The spacious and bright rooms offer you all the necessary comforts: safe, ironing board, selection of coffee and tea, as well as free Wifi and access to a Chromecast service. Personalize your stay and treat yourself to a room with a shower, bath or balcony. A preference for a high floor? it is also possible! Finally, connecting family rooms and suites will be an undeniable asset for the organization of your family trip or weekend.
The hotel is also known for the quality of its bedding, a good night’s sleep awaits you.
The restaurant - bar offers a friendly experience with its superb room, its central bar and its large terrace facing the park. Throughout the day, the Chef prepares a varied menu with Italian flavours: dishes to share, daily menu... you will be delighted! Enjoy a refreshing cocktail and antipasti and enjoy a relaxing moment.
If you are looking for something to do after a busy day, you can choose between a fitness centre, a walk along the Rhone River or a race in the park with its entrance facing the hotel.
