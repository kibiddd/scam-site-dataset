The 1999 Kansas City Royals played 161 games during the regular season, won 64 games, lost 97 games, and finished in fourth position. They played their home games at Kauffman Stadium (Park Factors: 101/101) where 1,506,068 fans witnessed their 1999 Royals finish the season with a .398 winning percentage.
