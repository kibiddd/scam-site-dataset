Born in Westover, he was the son of the late Herbert Sr. and Lola (Holden) Armwood. His late wife, Beatrice Brown Armwood, died in 1981.He made his home in New York for 35 years, retiring from the city of New York maintenance department after 30 years.He was a member of the Grand Order of Masons.His survivors include one son, Randolph (Crystal) Waters; a grandson, Zachary, all of Orlando, Fla.; four sisters and one brother, Beatrice (Sidney) Hayward of Princess Anne, Mary (Fred) Cottingham of Marion Station, Mae Card of Westover and Garfield Armwood of Crisfield; a special nephew, Jimmy Dutton of Salisbury; and several nieces and nephews.Two sisters and two brothers, Elizabeth White, Lola Harris, Herbert Armwood Jr. and Edward James preceded him in death.A funeral service will be held at 2 p.m. today at Metropolitan United Methodist Church in Princess Anne, where friends may call one hour prior to the service.
