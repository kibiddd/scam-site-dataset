Klein1918 - 2011Send FlowersFollowShareBORN1918DIED2011Gladys Klein ObituaryGladys KleinGladys M. Klein, nee Wrigley, 93, of Collinsville, Ill., born Wednesday, April 3, 1918, in Caseyville, Ill., died Tuesday, June 7, 2011, at Edwardsville Nursing & Rehab, Edwardsville, Ill.Mrs. Klein was a member of Ss. Peter & Paul Catholic Church, Collinsville, Ill.She was preceded in death by her husband, Eugene Klein whom she married on July 4, 1938; her parents, George and Elizabeth, nee Bohnenstiehl, Wrigley; six brothers, Leroy, Earl, Clarence, Floyd, Jerome and Claude Wrigley; and a sister, Bernice.Surviving are her children, Craig (Marsha) Klein of Collinsville, Ill., Gary (Pamela) Klein of Summerland Key, Fla., and Donna (Leonard) Turnbull of O'Fallon, Ill.; nine grandchildren; and 11 great-grandchildren.Memorials may be made to Heartland Hospice or Edwardsville Nursing and Rehab. Condolences may be expressed to the family online at www.herrfuneral.comVisitation: Friends may call from 4 to 8 p.m. Friday, June 10, 2011, at Herr Funeral Home, Collinsville, Ill.Funeral: Funeral service will be held at 10 a.m. Saturday, June 11, 2011, at Herr Funeral Home, Collinsville, Ill., with Father John Beveridge officiating. Burial will be at Ss.
