Information
"OVER" is the seventh single released by Hey! Say! JUMP. It was released in three versions: a limited CD+DVD edition 1, a limited CD+DVD edition 2, and a regular CD-only edition. The single includes a special code to unlock a message from one of the ten members. Each member has three messages each, totaling to thirty different messages in all. "OVER" sold 265,390 copies in its first week, making it the highest first-week sales by Hey! Say! JUMP yet. It surpasses their previous personal best with "Ultra Music Power" by almost 20,000 copies. "OVER" ranked as the #15 single for 2011.
The covers were intended to be changed to exclude Morimoto Ryutaro due to his underage smoking scandal, but it was called off since the singles were already distributed to retailers.
The single is certified Platinum for shipment of 250,000 copies .
