Queenie was born in Pointe Coupee Parish on Sept. 19, 1946. Survived by her husband of 44 years, Dr. Richard C. Hebert. She was the beloved mother to Stephen Hebert, Denise Hebert, Jana and Michael Hebert and Anne Marie Hebert. Her grandchildren are Charlie Hebert, George Hebert and Cecilia Hebert. Preceded in death by her parents, Gertrude "Bootie" Hebert and Dr. C.E. Hebert Jr.; and brother, James Alcide Hebert. She is survived by siblings, Dr. C.E. Hebert III and wife Aline, Lucille and husband Dr. Larry McCaskill, Mary Katherine and husband Stephen Bergeron, and Louis Hebert and wife Grace. Visitation at Rabenhorst Funeral Home, 825 Government St., on Monday, Sept. 27, was from 4 p.m. to 7 p.m. Visitation at St. Mary's Catholic Church, New Roads, on Tuesday Sept. 28, from 9 a.m. until celebration of the Mass of Christian Burial at 11 a.m. Entombment at False River Mausoleum. Memorial donations may be made to St. Mary's Catholic Church, 348 W.
