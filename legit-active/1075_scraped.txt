Restoring Active Life.
Welcome to the beginning of your return to a pain-free, active lifestyle.
Proudly serving the communities in North Georgia and Tennessee Valley, the Center for Spine & Pain Medicine specializes in interventional pain management. Our trusted, board-certified physicians and dedicated team takes a comprehensive, holistic approach to discover and identify the underlying cause and source(s) of your pain and work with you to carefully craft highly personalized care and treatment plans focused on your individual situation.

Contact UsTreatments
We understand that no one likes to undergo surgery or invasive treatment options. We get it. That’s why our highly skilled physicians and medical team work together with you to ensure we review all options that will deliver the desired results that allow you to return to your normal daily activities. Depending on your case, we also seek to limit the use of prescription drugs, avoiding a reliance on them to “numb” your pain as well as any additional side effects that they may cause. Learn more about many of the treatment options the Center for Spine & Pain Medicine utilize.
We treat people, not patients; and that’s why our philosophy focuses on educating you and your loved ones about treatment options and best practices. Our goal to return you to your normal activities function while improving your quality of life.
Our Mission
Our Mission is to serve our community by delivering the highest quality pain management care in a professional and caring environment.
Technology & Education
Our dedicated medical team is committed to ensuring everyone we care for is receiving the latest medical advances. That’s why we actively participate in continuing medical education and review the advances in pain management and surgical technologies. This means when you visit us, you’ll receive the most advanced care and support from our physicians, nurses, and care coordinators. With the latest, most effective modalities and technology at our disposal. We are pleased to be a part of your journey to a pain-free lifestyle.
