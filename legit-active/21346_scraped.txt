The 1978 Oakland Athletics played 162 games during the regular season, won 69 games, lost 93 games, and finished in sixth position. They played their home games at Oakland Coliseum (Park Factors: 95/97) where 526,999 fans witnessed their 1978 Athletics finish the season with a .426 winning percentage.
