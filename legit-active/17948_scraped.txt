The Way", he also created/wrote/produced and directed his own local
cable access show "Lauf Wit Me" that was taken into development by
Fox/John Davis. After becoming a SAG/AFTRA member, William was cast as
a principal in a regional "Foster Farms" commercial, a national "Block
Buster" commercial, as well as other film/TV projects. He had an
opportunity to shadow Stephen Spielberg on Jurassic Park, Joel
Schumacher on Batman & Robin(while playing a friend of Batman George Clooney at the observatory), David Lynch on a Swedish Commercial, and Antoine Fuqua on "Replacement Killers".After observing some of the best directors in Hollywood, completing
several of his own film/television scripts, and creating reality shows,
William started his own production company "King Of The Capital
Productions, Inc. and 3 years later raised $1.5MM started a second
production/distribution company "Exorcism The Movie, LLC". William shot
his first 35mm full length feature film "Exorcism". William wore many
hats as the writer/director/producer/casting director/actor. Exorcism
had an 18 day shooting schedule and was completed in 17 days with
William as a First Time Director. Exorcism, a Educational Spiritual
Warfare Film was one of the 250+ films that met all qualifications for
the 2004 Academy Awards, however William's first film project was
"mistakenly" left off of the voting ballot. William wrote, directed, produced and filmed his television pilot (Models Around The World) at Universal Studios City Walk in 2008 which was sunk in the waters by the 2008 financial crash. William is now a member of
SAG-AFTRA/DGA/WGA, and soon to be PGA. William has since created over
