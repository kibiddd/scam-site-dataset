NEW YORK, Feb. 16 /PRNewswire/ -- Reportlinker.com announces that a new market research report is available in its catalogue:
The Top 10 Confectionery Companies
http://www.reportlinker.com/p0177858/The-Top-10-Confectionery-Companies.html
The global confectionery market was valued at approximately $147bn in 2008, an increase of 3.2% over 2007- Leading players are being forced to adapt to changing consumer trends, with aging populations and concerns over obesity prompting increased innovation in functional and healthy food and drinks. The global price of cocoa is expected to continue rising due to growing demand for premium/gourmet chocolates emanating from mature markets as well as strong demand from emerging markets such as China and India.
This report analyzes the global confectionery industry in terms of market size, key drivers and resistors, trends and the competitive positioning of the top 10 players in the global confectionery market. It includes profiles of the top 10 market players in addition to performance summaries of other major players. The current ranking evaluates confectionery companies based on their growth strategies and M&A activity, financial performance and likely opportunities and threats for the future.
