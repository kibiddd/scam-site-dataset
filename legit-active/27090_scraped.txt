What Makes Our Chocolate Unique.
Chocolate Pizza Company lets you give the good stuff! Our unique Chocolate Pizzas, Peanut Butter Wings and other treats are made with the highest quality Swiss-style chocolate in our Marcellus, New York facility. For a truly decadent texture and taste, we blend in English toffee that we make in-house in small batches from a local recipe that goes back 100 years. Just one melt-in-your-mouth bite and you know this is not ordinary candy aisle chocolate.
