About this propertyComfortable Accommodation: Rooms feature air-conditioning, private bathrooms, balconies, and modern amenities. Family rooms and terraces provide relaxing spaces.
Wellness and Leisure: The hotel offers a spa and wellness centre, sauna, sun terrace, and seasonal outdoor swimming pool. Guests can also enjoy free WiFi throughout the property.
Dining Experience: A family-friendly restaurant serves Greek and Mediterranean cuisine with vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and dairy-free options. Breakfast includes warm dishes, fresh pastries, and fruits.
Convenient Location: Located in Olympia, the hotel is 83 km from Araxos Airport. Nearby attractions include the Temple of Zeus (19-minute walk), Archaeological Museum (1 km), and Ancient Olympia (2 km).Distance in property description is calculated using © OpenStreetMap
