WHO WE ARE
About Us
Western Autobody Ltd was founded in 1944 and is a 3rd generation, locally owned and family-operated business. From humble beginnings in a small shop to a new state of the art 15000 square foot building, we have expanded to become the most modern and well-equipped repair facility in Regina.
We have extremely well trained, qualified staff with an exceptional attention to detail, unparalleled service and friendly staff. We repair your vehicle to pre accident condition. Your manufacturer’s warranty is protected with us.
We can handle frame repairs, body repairs, refinishing, suspension repair & replacement and glass repair & replacement to all makes and models of vehicles. We offer free estimates, lifetime workmanship warranty and personalized service.
