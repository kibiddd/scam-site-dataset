Locust Street Festival
Great news – the Locust Street Festival (previously known as First Wednesdays Street Fest) is back and more exciting than ever! We’re bringing the ultimate street festival series to Locust Street in downtown Walnut Creek.
Mark your calendars – join us from 5:30-9:00pm, May 14, June 11, July 16, and August 6.

Walnut Creek Downtown is a Business Improvement District that includes every business within the downtown districts’ borders. Its more than nearly 650 members include restaurants, shops, entertainment venues and service providers. Associate memberships are available for businesses outside of the downtown district.  Walnut Creek Downtown is a 501c6 non-profit organization.
