FORRESTERSend FlowersFollowShareFUNERAL HOMECarmon Funeral Home & Family Center301 Country Club RoadAvon, ConnecticutMaureen FORRESTER Obituary  FORRESTER, Maureen B.  Maureen B. (McKenna) Forrester, 67, of Simsbury, beloved wife for 46 years to Kenneth G. Forrester, died Monday (January 10, 2011), at her home, surrounded by her loving family. Born in Concord, MA, she was the daughter of the late Joseph and Gertrude (Mara) McKenna and lived in the Simsbury community for many years. She was employed at the Nuclear Metals Corp of West Concord, MA and Eriez Magnetics of Erie, PA before choosing a career as a homemaker, parent, and grandparent. She was a member of the Edgar Cayce's Association for Research and Enlightenment, and was a social member of the Simsbury Flying Club. Maureen enjoyed gardening, bird-watching, and being of service to family and friends. Besides her husband Kenneth she leaves three daughters Karyn Horn and her husband Jonathan of Canton, Cindy Forrester and her fiancé Brian Carroll of Plainville, and Judy Fullerton and her husband Gary of Limington, ME; four grandchildren Andrew Horn, Alyssa Horn, Carter Fullerton, and Kathryn Fullerton; three brothers Joseph H. McKenna of Florissant, MO, James McKenna of Rye Beach, NH, and Leo McKenna of Hanover, NH; two sisters Gertrude McKenna of Stowe, MA and Margaret Hawksworth of Newport, VT; and many nieces and nephews. She was predeceased by an infant son Thomas W. Forrester. Her family will receive friends Friday, January 14, from 5-8 p.m. at the Carmon Funeral Home & Family Center, 301 Country Club Road, Avon. Family and friends may gather Saturday, January 15, 9 a.m. at the Carmon Funeral Home & Family Center of Avon, followed by a Mass of Christian Burial 10 a.m. at St. Bernard's Church, 7 Maple Street, Tariffville, with dear friend, the Rev. Timothy Meehan as principal celebrant. Burial will follow in Simsbury Cemetery. Contributions may be made to either Simsbury Volunteer Ambulance, PO Box 301, Simsbury, 06070 or the Farmington Valley VNA, 8 Old Mill Ct, Simsbury, 06070.
