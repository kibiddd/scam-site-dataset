American

Dale Eldred was an American Postwar & Contemporary artist who was born in 1933. How much does a Dale Eldred cost?Dale Eldred's work has been offered at auction multiple times, with realized prices ranging from 130 USD to 1,000 USD, depending on the size and medium of the artwork.What is Dale Eldred's most expensive sculpture? Since 2010 the record price for this artist at auction is 1,000 USD for Model for Landscape, sold at Christie's New York in 2010. Where can I see Dale Eldred's works?Spencer Museum of Art, University of Kansas featured Dale Eldred's work in the past. The artist died in 1993.
