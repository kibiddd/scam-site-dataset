George, devoted husband of Ida (Evans) for 54 years and dedicated father to Paul, his wife Gwynn and Pamela and her husband Mick. An accountant by trade and a poet by nature, he retired from Atlas Alloys in March of 1992. The most recent joys of his life were his 3 grandchildren: Samantha, Connor and Evan. He leaves behind 2 sisters, sisters-in-law and brothers-in-law. He is fondly remembered as 'Uncle George' to Shawnda and many other nieces and nephews both in Toronto and Ireland.
