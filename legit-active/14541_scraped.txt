Eastvaal Approved Used – Quality You Can Trust
What is an Eastvaal Approved Used Vehicle?
At Eastvaal Motor Group, we take the uncertainty out of buying a pre-owned
vehicle.
Our OEM Approved Used program ensures that every car meets strict manufacturer-approved
standards, so you can drive away with confidence.
Manufacturer-Approved Certification
Full-Service History Provided
Multi-Point Quality Inspection
Nationwide Delivery Available
Why Choose Eastvaal Approved Used?
OEM Approved & Dealer Certified
Every vehicle in our Approved Used program has been thoroughly
inspected and meets Original Equipment Manufacturer (OEM) standards. This means the car is
backed by the brand you trust.
Comprehensive Multi-Point Inspection
Each vehicle undergoes a detailed multi-point check, ensuring
that
key safety, mechanical, and performance standards are met before it is listed for sale
Full-Service History & Verified Mileage
Transparency is key. Every Approved Used vehicle comes with a
full-service history and verified mileage, so you know exactly what you're buying.
Nationwide Delivery
No matter where you are in South Africa, we can deliver your
Approved Used car directly to you.
Trade-Ins Welcome
Looking to upgrade? We offer competitive trade-in options,
making
it easier for you to switch to an OEM Approved Used vehicle.
