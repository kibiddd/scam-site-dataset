Since  2003, Wooden Wine Box Company has been one of the UK’s leading wine gift suppliers,  both to corporate and individual customers.   We have built our business on the simple premise of providing top quality wines,  gift boxes and service, all at fair prices.    Whilst we’re large enough to manage orders of thousands of gifts, we  are  small enough to offer a personal service.  So whether you want to  send multiple gifts in  branded boxes or an individual gift, you’ve come  to the right place.
Hopefully you’ll find the site easy to navigate, but we’re only ever a  telephone call away to offer help and advice.
