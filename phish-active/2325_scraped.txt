Apartments Kaludjerovic
The apartments are located in a villa on the promenade, at a distance of about 350 m from the sea, in the center of Ulcinj. All apartments are air-conditioned, have a spacious terrace overlooking the sea and the promenade, bathroom, kitchen, wireless Internet, satellite and cable TV, as well as a lift. Also, the apartments have their own private parking and has its own water tanks. Nearby, there are banks, restaurants, bars, beauty salons, boutiques, various shops and more. Kaludjerovic Apartments are equipped with comfortable and luxuriously furnished apartments intended for your comfortable stay. Kaludjerovic Apartments are the perfect place for families and friends who want complete comfort.
