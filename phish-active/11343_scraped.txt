About Us
Hotel Aagman Yatri Niwas is conveniently located in the centre of the city, within walking distance from Raja Ki Mandi Rly. Station and 8 Km radius from Taj Mahal and 7 Km from Agra Fort. The hotel offers excellent leisure facilities and high standards of comfort.
The hotel is exquisitely designed with 20 guestrooms to fulfill the guest's requirements. At Hotel Aagman Yatri Niwas, attention to detail and contemporary touches to all the rooms and bring out the best in luxury, style and comfort.
A heaven of peace and tranquility for business and leisure travellers, Hotel Aagman Yatri Niwas is located in the heart of the Agra, with easy access to Railway station, Agra Fort, Cinemas etc.
