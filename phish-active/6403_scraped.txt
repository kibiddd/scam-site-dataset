Drive in a clean air zone
Use this service if you’re planning to drive through clean air zones in:
Bath
Birmingham
Bradford
Bristol
Portsmouth
Sheffield
Tyneside - Newcastle and Gateshead
There’s a different way to check your vehicle if you want to drive in London’s low or ultra low emission zones or a low emission zone in Scotland.
You might need to pay to drive in a clean air zone if your vehicle does not meet emissions standards.
Use this service to:
find out if you need to pay for your vehicle
pay the daily charge
view maps of clean air zones
This service cannot check vehicles that are registered outside the UK.
Start now
Before you start
You’ll need the number plate (registration number) of the vehicle you want to check.
If you’re paying the charge for a single vehicle, you’ll need a credit or debit card.
If you’re a business and want to check a number of vehicles you’ll need:
an account - you can create one when you use this service
your company name
an email address
a spreadsheet in CSV format of the number plates (registration numbers) of the vehicles you want to check
If you’re a business and want to pay for a number of vehicles you’ll need a credit card, debit card or bank details.
Contact clean air zone support
You can get support if you cannot use the online service or you need help.
