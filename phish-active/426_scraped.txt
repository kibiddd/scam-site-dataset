Description
Benzcraft is a new way of playing creative plots. Worldedit is free for everyone. and can be used indefinitely. It’s a server where you can build a huge mountain in seconds *or* create the most immersive roleplay.
We decided to introduce a new way of playing-without paying. You get 4 huge plots for free. Plus unique tools to build what you desire or create that RP that you just never could because *you* needed to buy a rank.
We also designed it in a way we'd want to play. Thus the simplest yet most capable server was born.
the amount of commands needed to use while playing has been reduced heavily.
Our belief is that a player should be able to build to their hearts content. NOT to be slowed down because of complex commands.
Or a lack of access to the tools they need.
IP: benzcraft.net
~The Team
