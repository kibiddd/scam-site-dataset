WELCOME
Welcome to Audience Upholstery – a complete upholstery business based in Trowbridge, Wiltshire. We serve the whole of the UK, offering re-upholstery and refurbishment packages to venues such as schools, hotels, restaurants and theatres. We can also provide subcontract upholstery services to manufacturers, whether on a regular or a one-off basis.
With a multi-skilled team of specialist upholsterers, we pride ourselves on being friendly, agile and responsive to your needs. Contact us today to find out how we can help you.
